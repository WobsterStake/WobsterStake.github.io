Who We Are

We support the mission and objectives of Cardano becoming the world's most used blockchain solution. By operating a stake pool we continue to help secure and decentralize the Cardano network every day.

We have over 8 years of cyber security experience, IT operations and engineering. Protecting the node is taken seriously. We have a mix of bare metal and cloud technologies working together to bring together a seamless experience.

What is Cardano?

Cardano is the world's leading proof of stake blockchain solution, designed from first principles and independently peer reviewed by industry experts. This science first philosophy never seen in the cryptocurrency space before has created long lasting solid foundations for growth enabling rapid innovation.

Cardano provides a scalable, efficient, decentralized means for companies and individuals to build and govern DAPPS, systems and societies of unparalleled security in a sustainable way.
